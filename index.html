About

When you reinstall your operating system or change you computer you lose all your chats history. Skype Historian allows you to easily backup all your Skype chats to files in different formats.

Skype Historian allows to export your chats into the following formats:

  • Text Files (*.txt)
  • JavaScript Object Notation
  • Comma-separated values
  • XML

Additionally, files can be compressed into a single ZIP archive.
